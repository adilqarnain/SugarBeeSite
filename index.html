About SugarBee

SugarBee was founded by people with diabetes who understand the daily challenges of managing this condition. We believe that diabetes management shouldn't be complicated or overwhelming.

Our mission is simple: to create tools that make living with diabetes easier, more intuitive, and less stressful. Every feature in our app is designed with real user needs in mind, based on feedback from our community of millions of users worldwide.

From simple glucose logging to advanced analytics and device integration, SugarBee grows with you on your diabetes management journey.
